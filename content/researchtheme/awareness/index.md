We are interested in how we can help human stakeholders
(operators, observers, etc.) have appropriate understanding
of robots and their situations. This requires us to design
methods (such as visualizations) that help communicate robot
state, environment, plans, and history to users. One aspect that
we explore is using robots to provide viewpoints (move cameras)
to help observe robots (or other aspects of the environments).
