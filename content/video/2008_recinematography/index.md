This video demonstrates Re-Cinematography - our approach for post-process video stabilization that tries to produce high-quality camera motions. The [original paper](https://graphics.cs.wisc.edu/Papers/2007/GL07/) (at MultiMedia 2007) won the "best in track award," and was invited to be extended as a journal paper in [TOMCCAP](https://graphics.cs.wisc.edu/Papers/2008/GL08/).

Note: this is taken from a "FLV" file so, at one point it was highly compressed using 2007 technology. It was before we had reliable H.264 (or better).
